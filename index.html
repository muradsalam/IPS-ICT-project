Welcome to Spain

Spain is a country in Europe. It is known for its rich culture and history. Spain is a popular tourist destination, with millions of people visiting each year. The country is home to beautiful beaches, stunning architecture, and delicious food. Whether you are interested in exploring historic cities, relaxing on the beach, or trying new foods, Spain has something for everyone.

Popular Destinations

  • Barcelona
  • Madrid
  • Seville
  • Valencia
  • Granada

Things to Do

  • Visit the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona
  • Explore the Prado Museum in Madrid
  • See a flamenco show in Seville
  • Relax on the beaches of Valencia
  • Visit the Alhambra in Granada
